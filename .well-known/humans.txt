  - Version: Stable (2021 edition)

  - Version: 0.8
  - Purpose: Turtle parsing, graph storage
  - URL: https://docs.rs/sophia/

  - Version: 0.2
  - Purpose: WASM bindings
  - URL: https://rustwasm.github.io/docs/wasm-bindgen/

  - Version: 11+

- Semantic web first
- Accessibility mandatory (WCAG 2.3 AAA)

- Progressive enhancement
- Political neutrality
- Emotional safety for contributors

- Transparent decision-making
